You might have noticed, the favicon on this web page. I have made this icon responsive to colour. This means that if the device is on dark mode, a light yellow colour icon will show. For devices on the normal day mode, the same icon will show in dark blue. This works for both: mobile phones and on computers. Switch between day and night mode and try it out yourself!

I thought I'll do this to give you a better understanding on how a responsive favicon works.
